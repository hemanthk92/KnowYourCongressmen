How does your Congressional Representative compare?

Missing Vote Predictor

Purpose

The goal of this web app is create tools that make analyzing and visualizing a congressmen's voting record easier. Voting data is so exhaustive and intricate that data science methods are extremely helpful in aggregrating and understanding the data.


Methodology

Voting Record Comparer

The Voting Record Comaparer is a tool to compare a politican's voting record to his/her's counterparts in Congress. The dots on the graphs represent different members of congress (blue for Democrats and red for Republicans). The y axis is the distance a particular congressmen is from the selected congressmen. The selected congressmen is represented with the green triangle and has distance of 0 to him/her self.

The voting records were extracated from the Govtrack.us API. The voting records were then vectorized and placed in a matrix. Distances are computed using Cosine Similarity.


Missing Vote Predictor

The Missing Vote Predictor, predicts the votes of a member of congress on all of the bills he/she has missed so far during the 114 Congress. The predictions are formed from the Non-Negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) of the matrix voting records.
